INTERPRETATION
Germline testing across 12 hereditary breast and ovarian cancer genes returns NEGATIVE for pathogenic and likely pathogenic
variants. Notably, BRCA1, BRCA2, PALB2, ATM, CHEK2, and TP53 are fully covered (mean coverage > 200x; minimum 50x per

risk-adapted surveillance of first-degree relatives per current institutional guidelines is reasonable.

Variants of uncertain significance, likely benign, and benign are not reported here unless directly affecting the requested decision. Large rearrangements assessed
