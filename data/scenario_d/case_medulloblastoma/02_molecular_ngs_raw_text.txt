INTERPRETATION
This pediatric CNS panel identifies a PTCH1 p.(Arg691*) loss-of-function variant (VAF 49%), a definitional event for SHH-activated
medulloblastoma, together with a confirmatory SHH-activated RNA expression signature. TP53 is wild-type (Sanger confirmed);
SUFU is wild-type. There is no MYC or MYCN amplification. The constellation supports an integrated diagnosis of medulloblastoma,
SHH-activated and TP53-wildtype per WHO CNS5 (2021). TP53 wild-type status stratifies this case into the favorable molecular risk
category within SHH-activated medulloblastoma (the prognostically adverse SHH-activated and TP53-mutant entity is excluded).

METHODOLOGY & LIMITATIONS
Targeted NGS of 172 CNS-tumor-associated genes performed on tumor DNA extracted from frozen tissue and FFPE block (B3). Mean target coverage 1,020x; minimum
250x. Analytical sensitivity approximately 5% VAF for SNVs and small indels. RNA expression centroid analysis performed on RNA from the same specimen using a
285-gene MB-centroid panel. Copy-number analysis derived from per-gene normalized read depth. The assay does not reliably detect chromothripsis, complex
rearrangements, or methylation events (reported separately via Illumina EPIC array). Helix Genomic Reference Laboratory validated this laboratory-developed test; it has
not been cleared or approved by the FDA.
